# Product Name

## Purpose

This document tracks the evolution of the product's name.

Names should emerge from a clear understanding of the product rather than being chosen too early.

The goal is to find a name that represents the product's identity, not simply its functionality.

## Naming Principles

The final product name should:

* Feel like a real software product.
* Be easy to pronounce and remember.
* Avoid overly technical or AI-focused terminology.
* Reflect the product rather than a single feature.
* Still make sense if additional integrations are added in the future.

Avoid names that are purely descriptive, such as:

* Meeting Prep Assistant
* AI Meeting Assistant
* Smart Meeting Notes
